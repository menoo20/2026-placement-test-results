Curriculum Recommendations

"Here are my recommendations on the current curriculum based on my observations of the students' performance last week:"

  • Too Condensed The current curriculum is 5x more condensed than the students' abilities allow.
  • Pacing Issue 90% of the students couldn't grasp the "Family Unit" (or any unit) in a single day.
  • Recommended Timeframe The minimum recommended time to teach a lesson is 5 to 7 days. Students need repetition, drilling, and exercises, plus time for teachers to find, create, and customize the material needed to meet lesson objectives.
  • Resource Support Needed A curriculum without material is very tedious for teachers. They should not be expected to work voluntarily on creating curriculum at home, as most teachers (like myself) work 10 hours a day. It would be helpful to find a way to provide ready-made material for them.
